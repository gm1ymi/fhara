About FHARA

The Fort Herkimer Amateur Radio Association was founded on May 17, 1978 as the Fort Herkimer Amateur Radio Club. The name was changed in 2001 to reflect our incorporation as the Fort Herkimer Amateur Radio Association, Inc.

As both an ARRL Affiliated Club and ARRL Special Service Club, we're committed to promoting amateur radio in the Herkimer County area. Our association serves approximately 55 members and welcomes anyone interested in amateur radio, whether you're a licensed operator or just curious about the hobby.

We meet regularly to share knowledge, work on projects, and support our community through emergency communications and public service events.
