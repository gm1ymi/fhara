üö® Emergency Alert System

If an emergency arises and you need assistance, sound the "Litz Tone":

Press and hold PTT + "0" for 5 seconds

After the two-tone alert sounds, state "this is" followed by your call sign and the type of assistance needed. A member monitoring the repeater will respond and can make calls on your behalf while maintaining radio contact.

Repeater Information

Emergency Power: Each repeater is equipped with an emergency power source for operation during commercial power failures.

Frequency Coordination: All repeaters have received frequency coordination through the Upper New York Repeater Council, Inc. (UNYREPCO).

Public Access: These repeaters are open for public use unless there is an emergency. During an emergency, you will hear a "long tone zero" (Litz Tone) followed by an operator announcement. Please follow all instructions given by the emergency operator.
